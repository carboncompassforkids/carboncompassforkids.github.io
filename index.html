🔥 Why Is Earth Getting Hot?

Our Earth is warming up because of too much pollution. This makes the planet wear a heat-trapping blanket!

We can help take off that blanket and keep Earth cool! ❄️

🌈 What Can You Do?

  • 🚴‍♂️ Ride your bike or walk
  • 🔦 Turn off lights when not using them
  • 🌱 Plant trees and flowers
  • ♻️ Recycle paper and bottles
  • 🚿 Take quick showers
