🤝 Amazing Organizations Helping the Planet

🌐 Climate Action Network

Climate Action Network is a global group of 1,900+ organizations united to fight climate change and promote sustainability.

🌊 Seaside Sustainability

This group protects our oceans and teaches kids how to care for coastal areas and marine life.

🏙️ Climate Resolve

They help cities prepare for climate change with smart solutions and community teamwork.

🌱 The Climate Initiative

TCI gives young climate champions the tools to create real change in their schools and communities.

🗳️ Citizens' Climate Lobby

This team helps people talk to leaders and make powerful climate laws happen.

🌍 Environmental Defense Fund

EDF uses science to solve climate problems and protect nature for everyone.

⚡ Climate Group

They help big companies and governments take bold steps to stop climate change fast.

🌐 World Climate Foundation

This group builds partnerships around the world to solve climate and biodiversity problems.

🌡️ Climate Reality Project

Started by Al Gore, they train people to speak up and act on climate solutions everywhere.
